# Crisis-Recovery-Insights-for-Quick-Bite-Express

**Domain:** Food Delivery & Consumer Analytics  
**Function:** Crisis Recovery & Business Strategy

**Company Overview:**  
Quick Bite Express is a Bengaluru-based food-tech platform (founded in 2020) that connects customers with restaurants and cloud kitchens.

**Crisis Summary (June 2025):**  
A viral social media incident involving food safety violations at partner restaurants, combined with a week-long delivery outage during the monsoon season, led to:

- Customer backlash  
- Sharp decline in monthly orders  
- Increased cancellations  
- Partner restaurants shifting to competitors  
- Rapid loss of trust and brand credibility  

---

Pre-Crisis Orders (Jan–May): 107,000  
Crisis Orders (Jun–Sep): 31,000  
Overall Decline: −70.9%

**Key Insights**

- Sharp decline at onset: Orders dropped ~61% from May to June.  
- No immediate recovery: Volumes remained at ~7,500 orders/month during the crisis.  
- Trust breakdown: Decline reflects loss of confidence, not only operational disruption.  
- Slow stabilization: Slight improvement in September, but still far below baseline.

**Strategic Recommendations**

- Launch food safety transparency campaigns.  
- Reactivate loyal pre-crisis users with personalized offers.  
- Strengthen delivery network contingency plans.  
- Monitor sentiment and recovery KPIs weekly.

- Decline is uniform across cities, indicating widespread reputation damage.

**Strategic Actions**

- Roll out city-targeted recovery campaigns, starting with Bengaluru and Mumbai.  
- Highlight certified safe partner restaurants in-app.  
- Track city-wise sentiment to guide localized messaging.

**Key Insights**

- Some restaurants experienced declines >85%, showing brand-level distrust spillover.  
- Declines occurred across cuisines → platform credibility, not food type, was impacted.

**Strategic Recommendations**

- Introduce Verified Safe Partner badges.  
- Provide booster visibility to previously high-performing partners.  
- Display inspection and hygiene ratings transparently in-app.

**4. Cancellation Rate Trends**

| Period | Cancellation Rate |
|--------|------------------|
| Pre-Crisis | 6.0% |
| Crisis | 11.9% |

**Insight**

Cancellations nearly doubled due to:

- Delivery delays  
- Hesitation to complete orders  
- Lingering post-crisis uncertainty  

**Recovery Actions**

- Improve real-time delay communication.  
- Analyze cancellation reasons in detail.  
- Reinforce safety, quality, and refund assurance messaging.

---

**5. Delivery SLA Compliance**

| Period | SLA Compliance Rate |
|--------|--------------------|
| Pre-Crisis | 44% |
| Crisis     | 13% |

**Insight**

Severe reliability issues caused by:

- Reduced active rider availability  
- Restaurant downtime  
- Lack of operational redundancy  

**Actions**

- Build backup rider and routing systems.  
- Implement real-time SLA monitoring dashboards.  
- Offer performance-based incentives for timely deliveries.

---

**6. Customer Ratings Trend**

| Month | Avg Rating |
|-------|------------|
| Jan–May | 4.3–4.7 (Stable, Strong Performance) |
| June | 2.6 (Sharp Drop) |
| Jul–Sep | 2.4–2.7 (Sustained Low Confidence) |

**Insight**

Ratings fell sharply and remained low, indicating emotional trust damage.

**Actions**

- Launch a “We’re Back & Better” recovery campaign.  
- Audit and remove consistently low-rated partners.  
- Enable automated recovery refunds for negative experiences.

---

**7. Customer Sentiment – Top Negative Themes**

Common review terms during the crisis:

“Poor hygiene”, “stale food”, “delivery was late”, “not recommended”.

**Interpretation**

Recovery must prioritize trust and safety assurance before promotions.

**9–10. Loyalty & High-Value Customer Drop-Off**

- Previously satisfied high-frequency users stopped ordering.  
- Top 5% high-spend users showed the largest decline in frequency and rating.

**Implication**

The crisis damaged emotional trust, not just operational reliability.

**Targeted Win-Back Strategy**

- Personalized “We Miss You” outreach with trust-focused messaging.  
- “Quick Bite Elite Comeback Program” offering priority service and exclusive rewards.
